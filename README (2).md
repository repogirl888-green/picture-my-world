# Picture My World 🧩

A photo puzzle game built entirely from my original photography — Newark skylines, sunrises over the Passaic, Georgia landscapes, autumn leaves, and the moon.

**Play it live:** https://repogirl888-green.github.io/picture-my-world/

## How to Play

1. Pick a photo from the gallery — puzzles are organized into themed packs:
   - **Newark** — the city I call home
   - **Skylines & Sunrises** — Manhattan on the horizon at dawn
   - **Seasons** — autumn color and changing light
   - **Southern Roots** — Georgia live oaks, fields, and country roads
   - **Moon** — my moon collection
2. Tap one tile, then tap another to swap them.
3. Rebuild the photo. The sunrise bar at the top fills as pieces lock into place.
4. Choose your challenge: Easy (3×3), Medium (4×4), or Hard (5×5).

## Built With

- A single self-contained HTML file — HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript
- No frameworks, no build step, no dependencies
- All photos embedded directly in the page
